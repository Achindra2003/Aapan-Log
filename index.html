Apni Bakri

Apni Bakri (G.O.A.T) a.k.a Pal ji (Ashwani naam hai shayad) aapan log ke creator aur VVIP. Inka naam har jagha mashoor hai, matlab college har staff member se parichit hai bhai apne. Teachers ke favourite student hai yeh aur inke contact mein khaafi bade log hai (jaise ki mai) isliye inke saath no maajak. Yeh kabhi galtiyan nahi karte hai galtiyan inhe karti hain. Inke upar koi tip apply nahi hota lekin :"Kya aapke saabun ke charcoal activated hain?".

Virus

Vijay Raj Soni mera ladla bac... mera matlab chota bhai. Naam bada, Darshan aur bhi bada aur... dil thik hi size hai (dimaag gutter se nikaalo). Apna personal K-Pop star (U.P. Branch) aur minor. Iski jitni taarif karu kam toh nahi hai lekin jyada bhi nahi hai... abhi baccha hai toh rehne do, khelne kudne ki umar hai abhi toh. Virus ke liye ek hi tip hai: "Bhai apni dukaan se thoda sona de do dena kya karoge rakh ke".

Jahil

Sahil Kumar Yadav mera paaltu baap aur apne kahani ka Duryodhan. Jahil sahab ne apna interest anime aur memes mein hi seemit rakha hai jab se kota ne isse choda hai (dono meanings applied hai). Apne bohot hi keemti insaan hain yeh, apne ko gigachad mante hain lekin inke kaarnaame kaafi hai inke taarif ke liye. Jahil ke liye ek tip: "Tera Rs.20 udhaar bacha hai".

Ujju Boy

Ujjwal Pandey(Panday) mera doosra bac... mera matlab bhai. Ujju apne kaam ke liye toh nahi lekin naam ke liye jaroor mashoor hai. 'Gentleman' inke kai 'dostani' inko describe karengi lekin yeh sirf ek insaan ko describe kar sakte hain : Rohit Sha.... matlab Virat Kohli. Apne ujju sahab bohot bade waale pankhein hai Kohli ke aur BCA se jyada cricket mein interested hai. Ujju boy ke liye tip: "Apni daadi kabhi mat kaatna".

Samosa Somesh

Somesh Kumar (formerly eco-friendly) mere sawari ka yaar hai (UU3 member) iss liye roast nahi karne waala tha mai lekin phir samosa khate hue chatni nahi mili. Samosa inka priye khana hai, ek baar mein 5 kha jaate hain yeh. Contact bohot hai bhai ke lekin utne hi log iske maje lete hai (verbally). Yeh Rohit Sharma ke ek laute fan hai aur cricket ke 14 hai. 'Banya' ke naam se inhe inke school ke dost bulate hai aur inke dostani inhe 'kaam' se bulate hain. Inka tip:"Tip inka covered hai lekin iske dost Shami ka nahi".
